Jiahui Gao, Zhou Yi,Gao, Philip LH Yu, Shafiq Joty, and Jiuxiang Gu

Unsupervised Cross-lingual Image Captioning

Research in image captioning has mostly focused on English because of the availability of image-caption paired datasets in this language. However, building vision-language systems only for English deprives a large part of the world population of AI technologies' benefit. On the other hand, creating image-caption paired datasets for every target language is expensive. In this work, we present a novel unsupervised cross-lingual method to generate image captions in a target language without using any image-caption corpus in the source or target languages...
