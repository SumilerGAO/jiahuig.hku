About Me

Hi! I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate of Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science (SAAS) in The University of Hong Kong (HKU), supervised by Stephen M.S. LEE and Yu Leung Ho Philip. I am also working closely and supervised by Lingpeng Kong from Department of Computer Science, HKU.

My current research interest is Natural Language Processing, including Pre-trained Language Modeling and Cross-modal(Vision-Language) Learning.
